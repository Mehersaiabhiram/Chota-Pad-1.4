Why Chota Pad?

Built differently, by design

No servers storing your identity. No data brokers. Just you, and your files.

No Accounts

We don't ask for your name, email, or phone number. Ever. Your files is your account — nothing more is needed. No sign-up forms, no passwords to reset, no onboarding emails.

Only You Can Read

Files are locked behind your personal vault. Without it, nobody — not even us — can list or open your files. Your vault is completely private and isolated from every other user on the system.

The Technology

Your files are hashed with industry-standard algorithms before storage. Files live in an isolated server folder keyed to your code hash — not your identity. We never store the raw code.

Transparency

Our Simple Policy

  1. 1

    We do not collect any personal information. No name, email, IP logs, or device fingerprints are stored or associated with your code or files.

  2. 2

    Your code is hashed using bcrypt before it ever touches our database. We cannot reverse or recover it — if you lose your code, access to your files is gone permanently.

  3. 3

    Files are stored in a private, isolated folder on our server. No file can be accessed without a valid session token tied to the correct code.

  4. 4

    We do not share, sell, or analyse your files or usage data with any third party, advertiser, or analytics platform. There is no tracking of any kind.

  5. 5

    Sessions expire after 24 hours for your protection. Simply re-enter your code to get a fresh session. Old tokens are rejected automatically by the server.
